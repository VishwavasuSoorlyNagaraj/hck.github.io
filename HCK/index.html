Follow HCK  

Fancy playing cricket in Oslo's scenic chill? Swing by the Helsfyr Cricket Club!

Whether you've just stumbled upon cricket in Oslo, or you're curious about giving it a whirl, we're your squad.


With us, it's cricket season all year round - yes, even when the city turns into a snow globe. Our winter sessions are your perfect escape from the cold, so if you're battling winter blues and craving some action, we've got you covered. We cozy up at the NCF Indoor facility on Saturdays during the frosty months and when spring rolls around, we take the game outdoors at Ekeberg - because who doesn't love cricket in the sunshine?


Got a question or need more chuckles? Don't be shy, reach out for more info!
