Grounds in Norway

Ekeberg

Located in the tranquil environs of Ekeberg Park in Oslo, Norway.
Ekeberg Cricket Ground boasts three distinct fields: Ekeberg 1, 2, and 3. Accessible via convenient public transportation.

Rommen

Situated at Haavard Martinsens vei 35, Oslo, this charming cricket ground may be small but it offers a delightful experience.
Easily Accessible via public transportation.

Stubberudmyra

Situated right next to IKEA furuset.
Easy to get via buses.

Bærum

Situated in Kolsås.
Easy to get via T-bane line 3.

Drammen

Situated in Drammen. We car pool to this ground.
Remember to bring water and chair.
